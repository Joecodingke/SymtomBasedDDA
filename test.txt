symptoms of typhoid: 
 Fever Gradually increasing over several days, often reaching up to 104°F (39.5°C) or higher,  
 Feeling very tired and lethargic, Body aches and pains, sometimes severe, 
 A rose-colored rash may appear, particularly on the chest or abdomen, 
 Stomach pain Often in the area around the belly button, 
 Not feeling hungry or wanting to eat,  
 Experiencing constipation, vomiting and diarrhea.
